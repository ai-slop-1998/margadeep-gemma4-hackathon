# Margadeep Overview

## Pitch

Margadeep helps autistic children and caregivers turn unpredictable real-world moments into supported, learnable experiences. It is a personalized support companion that helps reduce sensory overload, increase predictability, and improve caregiver support over time.

The system is built around four connected loops: a real-time support companion for in-the-moment guidance in online or high-stress moments, a preparation mode focused on helping children build independence in the real world through planning and rehearsal, a caregiver copilot for deeper analysis and recommendations, and a learning layer that continuously personalizes the experience after each episode.

## Core System

### 1. Child-Facing Real-Time Companion (The "Fast" Loop)

**Function:** Immediate, in-the-moment support during high-stress scenarios.

**Mechanism:** Uses multimodal input to actively assess the physical environment.

**Output:** Delivers the least intrusive, highest-impact calming action instantly.

### 2. Child-Facing Preparation Mode (The "Simulation" Loop)

**Function:** Real-world preparation, independence-building, and proactive anxiety reduction.

**Mechanism:** Pairs model-generated support with A2UI to dynamically generate interactive planning tools, visual walkthroughs, and roleplays that help children prepare for situations they may face in daily life.

**Output:** Provides safe, structured support so the child can anticipate challenges, understand what might happen, and practice how to respond before entering the real-world situation.

### 3. Caregiver Copilot (The "Slow/Deep" Loop)

**Function:** Analytical support, pattern recognition, and strategy generation.

**Mechanism:** Uses agentic retrieval over external trusted autism guidance and the internal Personalization Graph.

**Output:** Answers complex parental questions and recommends new, grounded support strategies.

### 4. The Learning Layer (The "Bridge")

**Function:** Continuous, privacy-first personalization.

**Mechanism:** Logs outcomes and updates the Personalization/Recovery Graph after every episode.

**Output:** Ensures all future interactions and retrieved strategies become increasingly tailored to the child's unique needs.
